Delicious Seafood Recipes!!!

Garlic Butter Shrimp

  1. Peel and devein shrimp.
  2. Sauté shrimp in garlic butter.
  3. Add lemon juice and parsley.
  4. Serve over rice or pasta.

Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon

  1. Marinate salmon with lemon, herbs, and olive oil.
  2. Grill until the salmon is cooked through.
  3. Squeeze fresh lemon juice before serving.

Lobster Tail with Garlic Butter

  1. Cut lobster tails and baste with garlic butter.
  2. Bake until the lobster meat is opaque.
  3. Serve with melted butter for dipping.

Shrimp Scampi

  1. Sauté shrimp in olive oil and garlic.
  2. Add white wine, lemon juice, and red pepper flakes.
  3. Toss with cooked linguine and parsley.
  4. Top with grated Parmesan before serving.

Crispy Fried Calamari

  1. Slice squid into rings and coat with seasoned flour.
  2. Fry until golden brown and crispy.
  3. Serve with marinara sauce.

Grilled Shrimp Skewers

  1. Marinate shrimp in a mixture of olive oil, garlic, and herbs.
  2. Skewer and grill until shrimp are opaque.
  3. Serve with a squeeze of fresh lemon.

Cajun Blackened Catfish

  1. Coat catfish fillets with Cajun seasoning.
  2. Heat a cast-iron skillet and sear the fillets until blackened.
  3. Serve with a side of coleslaw.

Salmon Teriyaki

  1. Marinate salmon in teriyaki sauce.
  2. Grill or bake until salmon is cooked through.
  3. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.

Clam Linguine

  1. Sauté garlic in olive oil, add clams, and white wine.
  2. Cook until clams open; discard any unopened ones.
  3. Toss with linguine and parsley.
  4. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil.

Grilled Tuna Steaks

  1. Marinate tuna steaks in soy sauce, ginger, and garlic.
  2. Grill for a few minutes on each side until desired doneness.
  3. Serve with wasabi and pickled ginger.

Buttery Lobster Risotto

  1. Sauté shallots in butter, add Arborio rice, and cook until translucent.
  2. Gradually add seafood broth while stirring.
  3. Stir in cooked lobster meat and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.

Spicy Shrimp Tacos

  1. Sauté shrimp with taco seasoning until cooked.
  2. Fill taco shells with shrimp and top with slaw and salsa.
  3. Garnish with cilantro and lime wedges.

Crab Cakes

  1. Combine crab meat, breadcrumbs, mayo, and spices.
  2. Form into patties and pan-fry until golden brown.
  3. Serve with remoulade sauce.

Miso Glazed Cod

  1. Marinate cod fillets in a mixture of miso, soy sauce, and mirin.
  2. Bake until the cod is flaky and glazed.
  3. Sprinkle with chopped green onions before serving.

Seafood Paella

  1. Sauté onions, bell peppers, and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add rice, saffron, chicken broth, and a variety of seafood.
  3. Cook until the rice is tender and the seafood is cooked through.
  4. Garnish with fresh parsley.
