Delicious Italian Dishes !!!

Spaghetti Bolognese

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté minced beef and onions in olive oil.
  3. Add garlic, tomatoes, and tomato paste.
  4. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
  5. Serve the sauce over cooked spaghetti.

Chicken Parmesan

  1. Dip chicken breasts in egg and breadcrumbs.
  2. Fry until golden brown.
  3. Top with marinara sauce and mozzarella.
  4. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Serve over cooked spaghetti.

Linguine with Clam Sauce

  1. Sauté garlic and red pepper flakes in olive oil.
  2. Add chopped clams and clam juice.
  3. Toss with cooked linguine.
  4. Sprinkle with parsley and Parmesan.

Osso Buco

  1. Season veal shanks and brown in olive oil.
  2. Sauté onions, carrots, and celery.
  3. Add white wine, tomatoes, and broth.
  4. Braise until the meat is tender.
  5. Serve with gremolata.

Shrimp Risotto

  1. Sauté shrimp in butter until pink.
  2. Add Arborio rice and cook until translucent.
  3. Slowly add chicken broth, stirring continuously.
  4. Finish with Parmesan and parsley.

Veal Saltimbocca

  1. Place sage leaves on veal cutlets.
  2. Wrap with prosciutto and secure with toothpicks.
  3. Sauté in butter and white wine until cooked.
  4. Drizzle with lemon juice before serving.

Lasagna

  1. Layer cooked lasagna noodles with ricotta, mozzarella, and meat sauce.
  2. Repeat the layers.
  3. Top with Parmesan and bake until bubbly.
  4. Let it rest before serving.

Italian Sausage and Peppers

  1. Sauté Italian sausage until browned.
  2. Add sliced bell peppers and onions.
  3. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Serve over crusty bread or pasta.

Calamari Fritti

  1. Coat cleaned calamari rings in seasoned flour.
  2. Fry until golden brown and crispy.
  3. Serve with lemon wedges and marinara sauce.

Ravioli with Brown Butter and Sage

  1. Cook ravioli according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté butter until golden brown.
  3. Add fresh sage leaves and toss with cooked ravioli.
  4. Sprinkle with Parmesan before serving.

Chicken Piccata

  1. Dredge chicken breasts in flour and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add chicken broth, lemon juice, and capers.
  3. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
  4. Serve over pasta or rice.

Vegetable Risotto with Saffron

  1. Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add Arborio rice and cook until translucent.
  3. Stir in white wine and saffron-infused broth.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and Parmesan.

Salmon Piccata

  1. Sauté salmon fillets until browned.
  2. Add chicken broth, lemon juice, and capers.
  3. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
  4. Serve with a side of pasta or vegetables.

Gnocchi with Pesto Sauce

  1. Cook gnocchi according to package instructions.
  2. Toss with homemade pesto sauce.
  3. Serve with a sprinkle of pine nuts and Parmesan.

Italian Meatball Sub

  1. Make meatballs with ground beef and Italian seasoning.
  2. Bake until cooked through.
  3. Place in a sub roll with marinara sauce and melted cheese.
  4. Broil until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
