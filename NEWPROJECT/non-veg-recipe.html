Delicious Non-Veg Dishes !!!

Spicy Chicken Curry

  1. Marinate chicken with spices.
  2. Sauté onions and garlic.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato puree and simmer.
  5. Finish with fresh coriander.

Grilled BBQ Ribs

  1. Season ribs with BBQ rub.
  2. Grill low and slow until tender.
  3. Baste with BBQ sauce during grilling.
  4. Let them rest before serving.

Shrimp Scampi

  1. Sauté shrimp in garlic butter.
  2. Add white wine and lemon juice.
  3. Toss with cooked pasta.
  4. Garnish with parsley and parmesan.

Lamb Biryani

  1. Marinate lamb with spices and yogurt.
  2. Sauté onions and spices.
  3. Cook lamb and rice together.
  4. Garnish with fried onions and mint.

Beef Stroganoff

  1. Sauté beef strips with onions and mushrooms.
  2. Add beef broth and sour cream.
  3. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
  4. Serve over egg noodles.

Salmon Teriyaki

  1. Marinate salmon in teriyaki sauce.
  2. Grill or bake until cooked.
  3. Brush with additional teriyaki sauce.
  4. Serve with steamed rice.

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

  1. Cook chicken in Alfredo sauce.
  2. Toss with cooked fettuccine pasta.
  3. Garnish with grated parmesan.
  4. Season with black pepper.

Butter Chicken

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices.
  2. Sauté onions and tomatoes.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cream.
  4. Simmer until chicken is cooked.

Beef Tacos

  1. Cook seasoned ground beef.
  2. Fill taco shells with beef.
  3. Add toppings like lettuce, cheese, and salsa.
  4. Serve with lime wedges.

Chilli Garlic Prawns

  1. Sauté prawns with garlic and chilli.
  2. Add soy sauce and lime juice.
  3. Cook until prawns are pink and cooked through.
  4. Garnish with chopped parsley.

Pork Chops with Apple Glaze

  1. Sear pork chops in a pan.
  2. Make apple glaze with apple juice and brown sugar.
  3. Brush glaze on pork chops and bake until done.
  4. Serve with sautéed apples.

Fish and Chips

  1. Batter fish fillets and deep-fry until golden brown.
  2. Serve with crispy fries.
  3. Garnish with lemon wedges.

Thai Red Curry with Shrimp

  1. Sauté shrimp in Thai red curry paste.
  2. Add coconut milk and vegetables.
  3. Simmer until shrimp are cooked.
  4. Serve over jasmine rice.

Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

  1. Stir-fry beef with broccoli in soy sauce.
  2. Add garlic and ginger for flavor.
  3. Serve over steamed rice.
  4. Garnish with sesame seeds.

Cajun Blackened Catfish

  1. Coat catfish fillets with Cajun seasoning.
  2. Sear in a hot skillet until blackened.
  3. Serve with a side of coleslaw.
  4. Garnish with fresh herbs.
