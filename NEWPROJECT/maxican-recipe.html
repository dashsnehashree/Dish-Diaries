Delicious Mexican Dishes!!!

Tacos al Pastor

  1. Marinate pork with achiote paste and pineapple juice.
  2. Skewer the meat and grill until cooked.
  3. Serve in tortillas with onions, cilantro, and pineapple.

Guacamole

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes, onions, cilantro, and lime juice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Serve with tortilla chips.

Enchiladas Verdes

  1. Prepare a green salsa with tomatillos, jalapenos, and cilantro.
  2. Roll tortillas with shredded chicken and cheese.
  3. Cover with the green salsa and bake until bubbly.
  4. Top with sour cream and serve.

Chiles Rellenos

  1. Roast and peel poblano peppers.
  2. Stuff with cheese or meat.
  3. Dip in egg batter and fry until golden.
  4. Serve with tomato sauce.

Carnitas

  1. Slow-cook pork with citrus and spices.
  2. Shred and crisp in the oven.
  3. Serve in tacos or burritos.
  4. Top with salsa and onions.

Quesadillas

  1. Fill tortillas with cheese and optional ingredients.
  2. Cook on a griddle until cheese melts.
  3. Serve with guacamole and sour cream.

Pozole

  1. Simmer hominy and pork in a seasoned broth.
  2. Serve with radishes, cabbage, and lime.
  3. Top with chopped onions and cilantro.

Tamales

  1. Prepare masa dough and fill with meat or beans.
  2. Wrap in corn husks and steam until cooked.
  3. Serve with salsa and sour cream.

Mole Poblano

  1. Toast and blend various chilies, chocolate, and spices.
  2. Cook with chicken or turkey until thickened.
  3. Serve over rice or tortillas.

Ceviche

  1. Marinate raw fish or shrimp in lime juice.
  2. Add tomatoes, onions, cilantro, and jalapenos.
  3. Serve chilled with tortilla chips.

Sopes

  1. Form thick tortillas and create a raised edge.
  2. Top with beans, meat, lettuce, and salsa.
  3. Garnish with cheese and sour cream.

Chimichangas

  1. Fill flour tortillas with shredded beef or chicken.
  2. Fry until golden and crispy.
  3. Top with guacamole and pico de gallo.

Chilaquiles

  1. Fry tortilla chips until crispy.
  2. Cook with red or green salsa and shredded chicken.
  3. Top with cheese, sour cream, and avocado.

Tostadas

  1. Top flat tortillas with refried beans or meat.
  2. Add lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and salsa.
  3. Garnish with guacamole and sour cream.

Margarita

  1. Combine tequila, triple sec, and lime juice.
  2. Shake well and strain into a salt-rimmed glass.
  3. Enjoy responsibly!
